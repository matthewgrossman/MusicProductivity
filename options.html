Welcome to restraint! Think you waste too much time on a certain website? Add it to the blacklisted urls to the text area below! On the right hand side, choose which punishments you want to receive after wasting time. You can toggle productivity mode by clicking the icon in the top right. Remember to click save after modifying the blacklisted sites or punishments!
